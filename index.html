Prarie Creek Library

About

Luis Robles, employed by Meza Engineering at the time, contributed with the MEP systems design for Prairie Creek Library, one of the first buldings to achieve LEED Platinum certification in Dallas, Texas in 2012. The electrical system design integrate an on-site 7.7kw photovoltaic array. The building cooling and heating needs are provided by the geothermal/heat pump system. A daylight and artificial light system integrate for minimal energy consumption. Water-efficient plubming fixtures reduce the burden on municipal water supply and wastewater systems. Greywater and HVAC condensate are recaptured and reused.

Library Benchmarks

Compared to a baseline case

  • 32%less energy

    Energy analysis performed with Trace 700

  • 56%less fossil fuels

    Green power and on-site renable energy

  • 35%less potable water
